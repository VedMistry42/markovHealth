MOUNT SINAI COMPREHENSIVE CANCER CENTER
TRIAL PROTOCOL & INCLUSION GUIDELINES
Protocol ID: ONCO-KRAS-001 (Phase II)

TITLE:
A Phase 2 Study of Targeted KRAS G12C Inhibition in Advanced Non-Small Cell Lung Cancer (NSCLC) with Geographic Logistics Sub-study.

PRIMARY OBJECTATIVE:
Assess the objective response rate (ORR) of a novel KRAS inhibitor in heavily pre-treated individuals with NSCLC.

INCLUSION CRITERIA:
1. Histologically confirmed Stage III or Stage IV Non-Small Cell Lung Cancer (NSCLC).
2. Must have confirmed KRAS G12C mutation via liquid biopsy or tissue NGS.
3. Eastern Cooperative Oncology Group (ECOG) performance status of 0 or 1.
4. Patients must have progressed on or after standard-of-care platinum-based chemotherapy.
5. No symptomatic brain metastases. Brain mets must be fully treated and stable.
6. Willingness to participate in remote biomarker tracking.

EXCLUSION CRITERIA:
1. Presence of EGFR or ALK genomic aberrations.
2. Active autoimmune disease requiring systemic therapy.
3. Prior exposure to alternate KRAS G12C targeted therapies.

LOGISTICAL PREFERENCES:
- Prioritizing patients requiring mobile collection units to assess localized disparity mitigation.
- Can accept out-of-state patients via hybrid clinic routing.
